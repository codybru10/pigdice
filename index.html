Pig Dice

Rules for the game

Objective

The goal of the game is to be the first player to score 100 or more. It is played with a single six-sided die. On a turn, a player rolls a die repeatedly until either a 1 is rolled or the player chooses to hold (stop rolling). If a 1 is rolled, that player's turn ends and no points are earned. If the player chooses to hold, all of the points rolled during that turn are added to his or her score. When a player has reached a 100 with their current roll, the hold button must be clicked to claim win.
